iCorp has declared bankruptcy

Dear valued customer,

We regret to inform you that iCorp and its subsidiary Bank of iCorp have been forced to declare bankruptcy.

This decision was made after careful consideration and analysis of the company's financial situation, which revealed a severe lack of liquidity, too many employees, and other complications that made it impossible for the business to continue operating.

We understand that this news may come as a shock to you, and we want to assure you that we have taken all necessary steps to minimize the impact of this situation on our customers. However, we must inform you that if you do not withdraw your funds from iCorp and Bank of iCorp accounts by Monday, 17th April 2023, your accounts will be completely emptied.

We deeply apologize for any inconvenience or hardship this may cause you, and we want to assure you that we are working diligently to ensure a smooth and orderly wind-down of our operations. We understand that this is a difficult and stressful time for our customers, and we want to provide as much support as possible during this transition.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your account or the bankruptcy process, please do not hesitate to contact us. We will do our best to assist you and provide you with the information you need.

Again, we apologize for any inconvenience or hardship caused by this situation, and we thank you for your understanding and cooperation.

Sincerely,

Chairman at iCorp/Bank of iCorp
